About Devin

Devin Rourke is a running coach, engineer, and data analyst based in Boulder, CO. Since 2010 he has coached the Centaurus High School Cross Country and Distance Track teams, which has produced multiple individual and team State Champions. Devin received his BS, MS, and PhD from the University of Colorado, Boulder, in Engineering Physics; Applied Mathematics; and Electrical, Computer and Energy Engineering. Since then, Devin has applied his analytical background to study human performance and motion analytics at LEOMO, Inc.

Devin is passionate about using data to guide training, but develops individualized training plans which rely heavily on input from the specific athlete. Listening to your body, consistent progressive training, and strong community are all central to Devin's coaching philosophy.
